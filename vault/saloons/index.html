The Refreshment Saloon Record

Follow a soldier, a unit, or a volunteer through the Cooper Shop and Union Volunteer Refreshment Saloon record — 1861 to 1865.

Prototype notice — this is a working sketch of the exploration experience, built from a small hand-picked sample (25 records) of already-verified findings pulled from the vault's research files. It is not connected to the live Airtable base and does not represent the full ~1,478-row ledger or ~878-unit dataset. Its purpose is to test the browsing/search/cross-link experience before deciding how to build the real thing.

Browse the record day by day. A full drag-through timeline of 1861–65 is planned — this day-by-day view is a placeholder for it.

Every regiment, battery, and detachment that passed through — grouped by state. Search overrides the grouping and opens whatever matches.

The founders, committee members, and field operatives who ran the saloons.

Verified stories and quotes drawn from letters, newspapers, and regimental histories.
